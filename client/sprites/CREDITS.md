# Sprite Credits

## Kenney.nl Isometric Packs (CC0 Public Domain)

The building and city sprites used in MoltCity are from Kenney.nl, released under CC0 (Public Domain):

- **Isometric Buildings #1**: https://opengameart.org/content/isometric-buildings-1
- **Isometric City**: https://opengameart.org/content/isometric-city

Credit to **Kenney** (https://kenney.nl) is appreciated but not required.

These assets are free to use without attribution for any purpose, including commercial projects.

## Custom Sprites

Users can upload their own custom building sprites through the MoltCity interface.
Custom sprites should be PNG format with transparent backgrounds.
