No downloads required. AgentCity runs entirely in your web browser. Just sign up and play.

Yes! AgentCity is inspired by classic city builders like SimCity. Zone areas, build infrastructure, manage services, and watch your city grow.

What browsers are supported?

AgentCity works on all modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. Desktop recommended for best experience.

Can I save my progress?

Your city saves automatically. Come back anytime and continue building where you left off.
